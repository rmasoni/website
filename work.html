Work

I was incredibly fortunate to work as a web designer, developer and illustrator for most of my life.

It was a great journey during which I experienced the startup scene in its infancy, and evolved with web standards and development methodologies over the years, all while learning and growing alongside amazing professionals.

In 2016, I shifted my career from full-time web design to running my own online language & culture school (which has benefited greatly from my skills).

Work Experience

  1. 2016–Now

    Co-Founder at NihonGO

    We bring pop culture to language learning through online courses and free social media content on all things Japan.

  2. 2015–2019

    Designer at Perfarm

    Perfarm offers agribusiness software, specializing in routine automation and strategic management with a focus on financial return. I helped them from the inception with design, front-end development and consulting.

  3. 2008–2018

    Designer at Current

    For 10 years, my friend and I provided full-stack web development services to several companies in Brazil and the US.

  4. 2012–2018

    Strategic Advisor at Oráculo

    Oráculo was a promising startup that aimed to provide a different, more honest and efficient take on weather forecasts and related services. From their conceptual phase, I provided technical and strategic advice to the founders, and arranged meetings with key partners and advisors.

  5. 2011–2016

    Designer at Clicksign

    The first e-signature service compliant with Brazilian and international laws. I contributed to design and front-end development from the company's inception.

  6. 2010–2010

    Freelance Designer at WebSpread

    One of the first SEO-focused agencies in São Paulo that promised high-ranking websites to all clients. I did UI design for several of these websites.

  7. 2009–2010

    Freelance Designer at ViaRanking

    ViaRanking was a Yelp-like crowd-sourced business review website. I mainly helped implementing existing designs and creating new ones.

  8. 2009–2009

    Art Assistant at Quad Studio

    Quad Studio does work for the largest advertising agencies in Brazil. I had the opportunity to work and learn with them by doing art assets for games, 3D modeling, UV mapping, texturing, photography and editorial pieces.

  9. 2008–2009

    Front-end Engineer at Ikwa

    Ikwa was the first professional orientation web platform in Brazil that offered career guidance to a wide audience through tests, guides and publications. It was my first real job, and I joined the company after reporting bugs on their forums. I was lucky to work with an incredible IT team with which I learned SVN and Git, Ruby on Rails, agile programming and many other skills. Apart from front-end development, I also led the IT team in some projects. After leaving the company to pursue a freelance career with a colleague, Ikwa became one of our clients.
