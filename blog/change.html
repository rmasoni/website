Change

2023-02-01

I used to hate change. I felt hurt by it. I thought it ruined everything.

With time, however, I realized that change is not only necessary but inevitable. Change is a fundamental part of life. Every single thing changes — all the time.

My past self took a stance against the very concept of change, but what it really meant was aversion to specific changes in things and people that I loved. I was satisfied and very used to them. I didn’t want them to be different.

It’s rarely easy, but if we are to enjoy life, we have to deal with the eventual losses it brings us, and most importantly, learn to respect change and try even harder to notice its beautiful impact on everything that exists.
