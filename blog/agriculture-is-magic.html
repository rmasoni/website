Agriculture is Magic

2025-02-06

For some reason, I had the idea of planting lettuce in a pot to always have it available. I found a tutorial on YouTube and discovered that it takes 60 to 90 days and considerable dedication for it to grow.

This made me wonder how it’s even possible to offer lettuce year-round to a huge metropolis like São Paulo. After some calculations, I figured you'd need an area equivalent to one-third the size of Central Park (100ha) to plant 10 million heads of lettuce.

That's a lot of space and too much work to only have it four times a year.

Farmers are wizards.
