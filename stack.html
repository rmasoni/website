My App Stack

I've been in the Apple ecosystem for almost 20 years, so my operating systems are macOS and iOS.

My main browser is Safari, and my code editor is Neovim (augmented by AstroNvim).

I use Obsidian as my D&D campaign manager and general notetaker, TextMate as an excellent find & replace tool, Eagle for storing visual references, and Brave for Chromium-dependent stuff.

I can't function without Raycast and 1Password.
